The Year of the Token Economy

From 1.2% in early 2025 to 61% in 2026, China’s open-source models’ share on OpenRouter exploded — a review of the path and future outlook

📅 2026-06-02 ⏱ ~8 min read 🏷️ Token Economy · OpenRouter · Chinese Open-Source Models
Token Economy OpenRouter Chinese Open-Source Models DeepSeek Qwen
⚠️ Core Data: In early 2025, the usage share of Chinese open-source models on OpenRouter was about 1.2%. By early 2026, that figure had surged to 61%. This was no accident — it was the resonance of technology, pricing, and timing.

1Share Explosion — From 1.2% to 61%

OpenRouter, as a platform aggregating APIs of global mainstream models, uses its usage distribution as an objective metric for model competitiveness. Before 2025, Chinese models were almost negligible. The turning point came in Q1 2025.

📈 Three Stages of Explosive Growth

  • Stage One (2025 Q1-Q2): DeepSeek-R1 was released, and the overseas developer community discovered the extremely high price-performance ratio of Chinese models, triggering spontaneous adoption.
  • Stage Two (2025 Q3-Q4): Qwen3, GLM-4, and others followed, leading to a surge in OpenRouter integrations and intensifying price wars.
  • Stage Three (2026 Q1): Chinese models established dominance in coding, reasoning, multimodal, and other fields.

2Why Chinese Open-Source Models

Chinese open-source models achieved explosive growth in a short time, driven by three core factors:

🎯 Three Driving Factors

  • Price-Performance Revolution: Chinese model APIs are typically priced at 1/10 to 1/50 of GPT-4, yet deliver comparable or even better results.
  • Open-Source Transparency: DeepSeek open-sourced model weights, allowing anyone to freely use and fine-tune them, dramatically lowering enterprise adoption barriers.
  • Rapid Ecosystem Iteration: Model series like Qwen and GLM iterate quickly, with significant improvements in each generation, maintaining technological competitiveness.

3The Business Model of the Token Economy

At its core, the Token Economy is a usage-based charging business model. Model vendors calculate revenue by multiplying API call volume by unit price.

"The core of the Token Economy is scale effect — the larger the model call volume, the lower the marginal cost. Vendors can keep profitability while continuously reducing prices, eventually leading to winner-takes-all." — 2026 Industry Observation

4Competitive Landscape and Future Outlook

By 2026, the competition in the Token Economy has entered the second half, shifting from pure price competition to a battle of comprehensive capabilities.

🎯 Key Takeaways

  • The share explosion was no accident: Chinese open-source models went from 1.2% to 61% due to a triple resonance of technological breakthrough (DeepSeek-R1), pricing revolution (API pricing), and open-source strategy.
  • Token Economy enters the second half: Price wars give way to competition in comprehensive capabilities (reasoning, multimodal, enterprise).
  • Open-source is the core competitiveness of Chinese models: DeepSeek proved that open-source + high performance + low cost can shake the closed-source hegemony.
  • Future directions: Reasoning (Agent), native multimodal, and long context are the three high grounds of enterprise competition.
