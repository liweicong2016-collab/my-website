📌 Core insight

"Price hike without losing volume" works for top AI models — completely opposite to the internet‑era "free first, then charge" logic.

  • Users' willingness to pay for highly efficient coding tools is extremely high
  • True programming capability is scarce; supply cannot keep up with demand
  • Cinderella Glass Shoe Effect: first‑mover advantage of tier‑1 models brings extremely high user stickiness
